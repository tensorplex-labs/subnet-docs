GroundLayer

Groundlayer is an institutional-grade OTC marketplace designed for the Bittensor ecosystem, enabling subnet token owners to raise capital through structured, onchain-enforced deals without spot selling. The platform aligns incentives among sellers, fund managers, and investors by standardizing deal terms, enforcing them via smart contracts, and rewarding fund managers based on assets under management and performance. This approach reduces market instability, eliminates counterparty risk, and provides transparent, auditable access to Bittensor subnet tokens for institutional investors, while allowing subnet owners to fuel growth and fund managers to scale through proven results.
