Nepher

Nepher Robotics is a simulation-first robotics development platform that leverages high-fidelity physics simulation and AI-driven workflows to accelerate the creation and deployment of autonomous robots. The platform enables users to define requirements in natural language, rapidly prototype using a vast asset library, and train reinforcement learning agents in photorealistic, physics-accurate environments powered by NVIDIA Omniverse. Nepher hosts decentralized robotics tournaments where participants submit trained policies, which are evaluated in standardized environments, fostering innovation through open competition. The platform also offers enterprise-grade security, a talent marketplace verified via simulation challenges, and regularly publishes open-source solutions to advance the robotics field.
