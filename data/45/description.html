Talisman AI

Talisman AI (Subnet 45) is an AI-powered financial reasoning agent designed to provide real-time trading insights for the Bittensor ecosystem. It continuously monitors social media, market, and on-chain activity to identify and explain significant signals, offering actionable recommendations for trading and staking directly within Talisman wallets. The system leverages a decentralized network of miners and validators to analyze and validate sentiment and relevance of social media posts, ensuring high-quality signal data. Its phased roadmap aims to expand from social sentiment analysis to comprehensive on-chain and market monitoring, ultimately delivering personalized trading suggestions to users.
