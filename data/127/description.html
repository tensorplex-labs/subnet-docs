Astrid Intelligence PLC operates at the intersection of AI and digital assets, offering an AI-powered platform that autonomously builds and scales digital communities. Their core product, Astrid, leverages AI assistants to automate content creation and community growth, converting intellectual capital into community value. The platform features a digital asset treasury with strategic asset allocation for stability and exposure, supporting the operational model. Astrid Intelligence also provides bioinformatics and data analytics solutions, empowering scientific and biotech innovation. The company is headquartered in London, UK, and is publicly traded on the Aquis Stock Exchange.
