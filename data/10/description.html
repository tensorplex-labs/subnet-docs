Swap

swap (sn10 on Bittensor) is a project designed to incentivize miners to provide liquidity to the TAO<>USDC pool on the Taofi platform. It implements a scoring mechanism where miners are evaluated based on the fees their liquidity positions have generated over the past 24 hours, encouraging active and effective liquidity provision. The project is open-source under the MIT license and provides clear instructions for installation and development setup, targeting contributors interested in decentralized finance and liquidity mining within the Bittensor ecosystem.
