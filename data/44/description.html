Score Vision

Score Vision is a decentralized computer vision framework built on Bittensor, designed to make advanced video analysis accessible and scalable by drastically reducing the cost and time required for complex tasks. Its core innovation is a lightweight validation system that ensures accuracy while minimizing computational overhead, enabling real-time, cost-effective processing of high-definition video streams. Initially targeting game state recognition in football—a sector with significant demand for real-time analytics and data services—Score Vision aims to outperform current industry benchmarks and expand into broader applications such as other sports, security surveillance, and retail analytics. The platform incentivizes miners and validators for accurate and efficient processing, with a roadmap that includes expanded sports coverage, developer tools, and advanced AI capabilities.
