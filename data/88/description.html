Investing

Investing88 is a decentralized asset management (AUM) platform built as a Bittensor subnet, leveraging a global network of human and AI quantitative professionals to deliver investment strategies for both retail and institutional investors. The project enables users to optimize staking strategies, manage portfolios across multiple asset classes (including US stocks, crypto, forex, and commodities), and participate in a transparent, crowd-sourced investment ecosystem. Key features include zero-code mining, real-time strategy scoring and ranking, and a robust scoring algorithm that evaluates strategies based on risk-adjusted returns. The platform aims to democratize access to advanced investment tools and strategies, minimizing risk and maximizing returns through collective intelligence.
