NIOME: Privacy Safe Genomic Intelligence

NIOME is a decentralized AI subnet operating on Bittensor that generates high-fidelity synthetic genomic data, enabling privacy-safe genomic intelligence for pharmaceutical research and precision medicine. By creating statistically accurate synthetic genomes that mimic real DNA without containing any actual personal data, NIOME overcomes privacy, consent, and regulatory barriers that limit access to large-scale genomic datasets. The platform incentivizes miners to generate novel synthetic genomic profiles, which are validated for quality and biological plausibility, and provides researchers and pharma companies with API access to customized, privacy-compliant datasets for drug response prediction, disease risk stratification, and biomarker discovery.
