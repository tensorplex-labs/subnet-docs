BitAds

Bitkoop is a decentralized, community-powered coupon platform that transforms the process of finding online discount codes by incentivizing users (miners) to discover and submit valid coupons, which are then independently verified by validators. This system ensures only working, up-to-date discount codes are available, addressing common issues of expired or fake offers found on traditional coupon sites. Bitkoop leverages affiliate commissions for revenue, aiming to deliver a transparent, trustworthy alternative in the multi-billion-dollar coupon industry, while rewarding contributors and maintaining a self-sustaining ecosystem.
