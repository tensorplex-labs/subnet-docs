Cartha (0xMarkets)

0xMarkets is a decentralized trading platform aiming to make powerful financial markets accessible to everyone. Its liquidity is provided by Cartha, a subnet that enables users to become miners by locking funds into trading pools and earning rewards. The official Cartha CLI tool streamlines the process of registering as a miner, managing multiple pools, tracking balances and expirations, and securely authenticating with Bittensor wallets. The platform emphasizes ease of use, instant status updates, and robust management features for liquidity providers, supporting both testnet and mainnet environments.
