Trajectory RL

trajectoryrl is a Bittensor subnet (subnet 11) providing reinforcement learning as a service, where miners compete to optimize AI agent policy packs for real-world operational tasks. Validators deterministically evaluate submitted policy packs using fixed scenarios (via clawbench), rewarding those that are safer, more efficient, and more reliable. The project emphasizes safety-first scoring, deterministic evaluation (avoiding LLM judge randomness), and strong anti-copy incentives, including GitHub-based timestamp verification and a winner-take-all reward structure. Real-world scenarios include tasks like email triage, incident response, and task delegation, with a focus on making agent behaviors cheaper, safer, and more robust.
