sundae_bar

Sundae_bar is an enterprise platform that enables businesses to rent and deploy a single, continuously improving generalist AI agent capable of executing real-world business workflows across teams and tools. The agent is developed through an open, competitive process on the sn121 network, where independent developers submit open-source agents that are benchmarked and rewarded based on performance in structured business challenges. Revenue generated from commercial use directly funds developer rewards, ensuring sustainability and alignment with real-world value. The platform emphasizes transparency, open evaluation, and rapid iteration, aiming to deliver enterprise-ready AI automation while incentivizing ongoing innovation.
