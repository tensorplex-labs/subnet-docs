Bitstarter #1

Bitstarter is a platform that facilitates the launch of new subnets, allowing users to discover and support emerging teams that have been pre-vetted by industry professionals. Through Bitstarter, users can pledge support and gain early access to subnet tokens at pre-launch rates, effectively backing projects before their official launch. The platform emphasizes a curated approach, ensuring that only screened and promising projects are featured, and provides resources for users to learn about how the process works.
