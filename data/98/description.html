ForeverMoney 九八

sn98 forevermoney is a decentralized automated liquidity management protocol operating as a Bittensor subnet. It leverages competitive AI strategies to optimize liquidity provision on platforms like Uniswap v3 and Aerodrome. The system uses a jobs-based architecture where miners propose dynamic rebalancing strategies for multiple liquidity pools, and validators evaluate these strategies through forward simulations. Winning strategies are executed on-chain, with a reputation system rewarding consistent, high-performing participants. The protocol emphasizes inventory protection and value growth, requiring sustained participation for live execution, and is designed for advanced, intelligent liquidity management.
